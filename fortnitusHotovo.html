fortnite history

Fortnite Battle Royale is a free-to-play battle royale video game developed and published by Epic games. It was released as an early access title for Microsoft Windows, macOS, playstation 4 and Xbox One in September 2017, for iOS in April 2018, the Nintendo Switch in June 2018, and an Android version in August 2018. It is a spin-off from Epic's Fortnite save the World, a cooperative survival game with construction elements. Players start with no intrinsic advantages, and must scavenge for weapons and armor to gain the upper hand on their opponents.
